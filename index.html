About Us

Agranee Events is one of India’s upcoming events firm dealing in branding, BTL activations and events, with core belief of treating every project as a special campaign, it is spreading as one of the most reliable brand building solution providers in the market.

We have depth of expertise in branding and activation , boasts of having in its client portfolio more than 50 leading MNCs and corporate houses including Godrej Appliances, Godrej Interio, Godrej Smart Care, Safilo,Epson India, Essilor India Pvt.Ltd,Health & Glow.

We were founded in 2009, headquartered in ‘Pune’ has seamlessly worked towards becoming the synonym of client’s delight, With time it has evolved in expanding its reach in execution in major cities a namely; Pune, Mumbai, Surat, Ahmedabad, Indore, Bhopal, Lucknow, Delhi, Chandigarh, Amritsar, Jaipur, Hyderabad, Tirupati, Chennai, Bangalore, Virajpet & Kochi. We now offer services across all Indian cities via reliable associates.
